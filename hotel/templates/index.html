Our Features

The best restaurant in Mumbai keep with the cosmopolitan feel of this city, which blends a myriad of influences from all around the world, both in its history and its cuisine. Indian food itself has already been shaped by aboriginal, Spanish, North American, Indonesian and Chinese cuisines, so you’re almost certain to find something to your taste in Mumbai.

Mumbai's dining scene includes an eclectic list of eateries which only have 1 point in common: they're mostly are good. From upscale hotels restaurants to unpretentious budget cafés, and from local to international restaurants, the best places to eat in Mumbai are ready to serve you mouth-watering meals.

TCR Restaurant is a luxurious restaurant located in the historic Mumbai Hotel. It’s known for an extensive buffet selection that includes North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Punjabi cuisines and more. Their live cooking stations ensure that the food is well presented and of the highest quality. Sample their salad station, cold cuts and seafood station, burrito and shawarma station, and bread and cheese station.

TCR Restaurant is a stylish lounge and restaurant near the gateway of India. The venue is a stunning sundowner spot, offering views over Mumbai Bay. Food-wise, you can enjoy both Indian and international cuisine, served in big portions at fair rates. A live music band is in charge of the entertainment on some nights, but the highlight of the restaurant remains the sunset over Mumbai.
